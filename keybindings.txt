For smoother screen brightness change use these
bind = , XF86MonBrightnessUp,   exec, bash -lc 'for i in {1..10}; do brightnessctl -q set +1%; sleep 0.01; done'
bind = , XF86MonBrightnessDown, exec, bash -lc 'for i in {1..10}; do brightnessctl -q set 1%-; sleep 0.01; done'

Instead of
bind = , XF86MonBrightnessUp, exec, brightnessctl -q s +10%
bind = , XF86MonBrightnessDown, exec, brightnessctl -q s 10%-

Change sound by 5% instead of 1%
bindle=, XF86AudioRaiseVolume, exec, wpctl set-volume -l 1 @DEFAULT_AUDIO_SINK@ 5%>
bindle=, XF86AudioLowerVolume, exec, wpctl set-volume @DEFAULT_AUDIO_SINK@ 5%-
